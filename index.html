Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to Your Questions

What can I expect from a Mindful Astrology session?

Each session dives into your natal chart and current transits with compassion and clarity. You’ll receive practical recommendations, a recording of the session, and suggested rituals or practices to support integration.

How do somatic services complement astrology?

Somatic offerings help you embody the insight from your chart. Jai weaves mindful movement, breathwork, and energy healing to bring balance to your nervous system and anchor cosmic wisdom in your body.

Do I need previous astrology knowledge to work with Jai?

Not at all! Jai meets you exactly where you are—whether you’re brand-new to astrology or a seasoned star student. Sessions are collaborative, educational, and empowering.

Can Jai support ongoing transformation beyond one session?

Yes. Mind, Body, & Spirit Coaching provides ongoing mentorship, accountability, and personalized practices so you can continue integrating insight and creating aligned change.

How do I prepare for my appointment?

Bring your birth date, time, and location if you have them, along with intentions or questions. Consider journaling beforehand to connect with what you’re hoping to nurture or transform.
